What is Blackcoin?

Blackcoin is a decentralised digital currency with near-instant transaction speeds and negligible transaction fees built upon Proof of Stake 3.0 (PoSV3, BPoS) as introduced by the Blackcoin development team.

What is Blackcoin More?

Blackcoin More is a wallet for the Blackcoin currency enabling anyone to send and receive Blackcoin. Additionally, when running the wallet with staking enabled, you will receive new Blackcoins thanks to the original PoSV3 protocol.

As built on Bitcoin Core 0.13.2, a proven solid and secure base, it offers performance enhancements and wider compatibility with third party services than the original Blackcoin wallet. Blackcoin More has the full support of the Blackcoin community and is maintained on a regular basis.
